Part 1

Exodus 1-19

God's Unique Identity

  • Covenant-Keeping God

    Exodus 6:5 says God acted in Exodus because he remembered his covenant with Abraham, Issac and Jacob

  • Utterly Supreme

    Moses tells Pharaoh in 8:10: "Be it as you say, so that you may know that there is no one like the Lord our God."

  • Great Warrior

    Moses' song in 15:2-3 tells us that this is what God's defeating the Egyption army is all about

  • Caring Provider

    When Israel is hungry, God feeds them. Why? 16:2 "Then you shall know that I am the Lord your God

The Pattern of Redemption

  • Oppressed in Slavery

    The exodus is the salvation of God's people out of something: in this case, out of tyrannical captivity

  • The Lord Acts to Save

    The concept of redemption refers to purchasing freedom for a slave. The death of the Passover lamb is the ransom price for the firstborn sons of Israel

  • Lead to a Promised Land

    Israel is rescued out of slavery with the intent of taking them into a land where they can worship as God's people in God's place under God's rule.

Chaism (\kī-ˌa-zəm)

Chaistic structure is a literary device that looks like the left-hand side of an X. The symmetrical points are related, and often the central, or most important point is in the middle.

  • A:First Point
  •   B:Second Point
  •     C:Third Point
  •     C':Balances Third Point
  •   B':Balances Second Point
  • A':Balances First Point

Chiastic Structure

Noticed the mirrored/balanced themes

  • I. Ex. 20-23: Covenant Obligations (context)
  • II. Ex. 24: Covenant Ceremony (the promise to be with them)
  • III. Ex. 25-31: Covenant Meeting Place (tabernacle described)
  • IV. Ex. 32-34: Covenant Disobedience and Grace
  • V. Ex. 35-40: Covenant Meeting Place Built

Forward to

  • "I will dwell among the people of Israel and will be their God"
  • "And they shall know that I am the Lord their God"

Jesus

  • Jesus makes atonement for sins, once and for all
  • It is only through this atonment, that anyone can come to the Father
  • Jesus is the full revelation of God
  • God meets and reconciles with His people in Christ
  • Jesus is the manifestation of God's glory
  • Jesus is God in a physical body, dwelling with his people
  • Only through Jesus does anyone know God
