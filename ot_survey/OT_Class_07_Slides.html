What are the Psalms?

  • Collection of 150 musical poems & prayers
  • Entirely poetry

Who wrote the Psalms?

  • Moses - Psalm 90
  • Ezra - (maybe) 119
  • Sons of Korah - worship leaders
  • Sons of Asaph worship leaders
  • Solomon - 72
  • David - 73 total

How are the Psalms Structured?

  • Book 1 - Psalms 1-41
  • Book 2 - Psalms 42-72
  • Book 3 - Psalms 73-89
  • Book 4 - Psalms 90-106
  • Book 5 - Psalms 107-150

What are the Different Kinds of Psalms?

  • Lament
  • Thanksgiving
  • Praise
  • Enthronement
  • Royal
  • Zion
  • Wisdom
  • Trust
  • Liturgies
  • Torah

How do the Psalms Point Us to Jesus ?

  • Psalms about the Messiah
  • Psalms by the Messiah

How do we Read the Psalms?

  • With sensitivity to the Type, meaning, and location
  • Selectively as songs of the perfect God-fearing man
  • For ourselves through the Mediator

What are the Proverbs?

  • General principles and observations
  • seen in the created order by those who fear the Lord

How are the Proverbs Interpreted?

  • Determine the parallelisms
  • Identify any figures of speech
  • Summarize in your own words
  • Consider how to apply to your life

Four Themes of Wisdom

  • Contrasts to wise living
  • Context for wise living
  • Communication in wise living
  • The Outcome of Wisdom

Where is Jesus?

  • Describe the beauty of Jesus
  • Show us our need for Jesus
